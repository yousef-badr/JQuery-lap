Web design is a multidisciplinary field that encompasses the creation and maintenance of visually appealing and functional websites. It involves a combination of graphic design, user interface (UI) design, and front-end development skills to craft an engaging online presence.

E-commerce, short for electronic commerce, has emerged as a transformative force in the business landscape, revolutionizing the way people buy and sell goods and services. It encompasses a vast array of online transactions, ranging from simple consumer purchases to complex business-to-business transactions

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is a fundamental and dynamic digital marketing strategy that focuses on enhancing a website's visibility and ranking on search engine results pages (SERPs).
